HOW IT WORKS

  • 1. CLICK YOUR CHARACTER'S BUTTON WHEN IT'S YOUR TURN TO GET YOUR CHALLENGE.

  • 2. DO THE "DARE" OR ANSWER THE "TRUTH" TO MOVE YOUR HURRICANE CLOSER TO HOME
    AND YOU CAN PICK TO EITHER TAKE A SMALL SIP OF YOUR DRINK OR SKIP DRINKING.

  • 3. IF YOU REFUSE THE DARE OR SWERVE AROUND YOUR "TRUTH" ANSWER (ANSWERED WITH A "NON-ANSWER"):
    • - YOU WILL MOVE BACK A STEP.
    • - YOU HAVE TO TAKE A SHOT OR A BIG GULP OF YOUR DRINK.

  • 4. WINNER: THE FIRST PLAYER TO REACH HOME.

  • 5. LOSER: THE PERSON TAKING CARE OF YOU WHILE YOU PRAY TO THE PORCELAIN GOD.
